What is New?

  • New School Canteen

    The food options and responsibilities to consider,canteens must be be better organised and more efficient than they been in the past. Online menus with photographs of the meals prepared, and detailed descriptions of their ingredients help both students and parents make the right nutritional choices. Each student or staff member who accesses the canteen could be provided with a School Canteen Acount on the school website that keeps track of their previous meal choices and any sietary requirements they may have.

    • Implemented Online Attendance Management System

      Implemented Online Attendance Management System: This informs parents about their child's/children's activities in the school, instantly. Once a student reaches the school, the parents will be informed about the status via text messages. This helps schools keep a record of the students' absenteeism. Students can mark the attendance by logging in, and this is stored in a database, which can later be checked by the Admin. The tracker has so much potential that it can identify which students are irregular to their classes and not ready to establish connectivity with the actual concepts of their crriculum's subjects.
